Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence) at SVPCET is a vibrant academic community with a mission to drive innovation through teaching, research, and industry collaboration.

Faculty members are active researchers in areas such as Artificial Intelligence. The department emphasizes experiential learning through lab sessions, projects, hackathons, and internships.

With strong placement support, modern infrastructure, and industry tie-ups, our department nurtures talent and produces skilled professionals who contribute meaningfully to society.
